## LinkedIn post examples

### Post 1 — Restaurant automation

```txt
Most restaurant owners do not lose orders because food is bad.

They lose orders because:
- Phone is busy
- Staff forgets details
- Customer does not get status
- Table bookings are written manually
- Owner cannot see everything live

At AI Workflows, we are building restaurant systems with:
- Digital menu
- Online ordering
- Table booking
- Kitchen dashboard
- Owner dashboard
- Order tracking

A website should not only look good.
It should help the business run better.
```

### Post 2 — Cyber café AI form filler

```txt
Cyber cafés spend too much time typing the same details from customer documents.

Name.
DOB.
Address.
Father name.
ID details.

This creates:
- Long queues
- Typing mistakes
- Slow service
- Operator fatigue

We are building an AI Form Filler workflow where:
Customer scans QR → uploads document → AI reads data → operator reviews → form gets filled faster.

AI should solve real daily work, not just look fancy.
```

### Post 3 — Distributor software

```txt
Many distributors still manage orders through calls, WhatsApp, and notebooks.

The problem is not only order taking.
The bigger problem is control.

Who ordered?
Who approved?
Who delivered?
Was delivery confirmed?
Where is the proof?

A distributor dashboard can solve this with:
- Shop order portal
- Owner approval
- Delivery assignment
- Live status
- OTP confirmation
- Reports

Small businesses also deserve strong software systems.
```

# Final homepage quality checklist

- Hero communicates value in 5 seconds.
- CTA visible above the fold.
- Projects/demos are easy to access.
- Services are clear for non-technical clients.
- Pricing gives starting confidence.
- Mobile layout is clean.
- Animations feel premium, not distracting.
- Contact action is always obvious.

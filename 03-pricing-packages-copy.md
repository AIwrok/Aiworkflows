## Pricing FAQ

### Why final price depends on requirement?

Because a simple website and a full software dashboard are different projects. We first understand your business process, then suggest the right plan.

### Can I start small and upgrade later?

Yes. You can start with a website and add dashboard, automation, or AI features later.

### Is hosting included?

Hosting can be included or billed separately depending on the project plan.

### Is WhatsApp cost included?

WhatsApp API or messaging charges depend on usage and provider. We explain this before implementation.

### Do you provide training?

Yes. We explain the system to the owner and staff in simple Hindi/English.
